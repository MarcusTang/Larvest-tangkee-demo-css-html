Facilities

State-of-the-art Processing Facilities for Quality Assurance

The processing facilities of Guangzhou Guang Feng Foods Co., Ltd. are equipped with advanced technologies to ensure quality assurance. These state-of-the-art facilities include pre-cleaning, gravity selecting, destoner, magnetic separator, color sorter, dehulling machine, and retail packing, among others. Processing

Advanced technology for superior quality and safety

01

Removing hulls for cleaner, healthier grains and beans

Dehulling is a mechanical process used in grain and bean processing to remove the inedible outer hull from the edible seed inside. This improves the nutritional value and palatability of the product while producing a cleaner, easier-to-cook grain or bean.

02

Thorough and precise cleaning for superior quality and safety

Fine cleaning is a crucial step in grain and bean processing that removes impurities such as dust, stones, and other foreign materials from the product. Using advanced technology, the process ensures the purity and safety of the final product, resulting in a cleaner, higher quality grain or bean.

03

Reliable packaging for optimal freshness and convenience

Packing grains and beans involves selecting the right packaging materials and filling them to preserve freshness and extend shelf life. It is a crucial step to ensure customers receive a high-quality product that is fresh, convenient, and long-lasting.
